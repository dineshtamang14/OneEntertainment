About N-Pop

At One Entertainment, we're crafting a new genre: N-Pop. Rooted in Nepal's traditions, elevated with Bollywood rhythms, and expressed in bilingual lyrics, N-Pop bridges cultures and connects hearts.

Meet Our Star: Rohan Gurung

Rohan Gurung

Rohan is a 20-year-old solo musician from Kathmandu, known for his captivating bilingual performances that merge Nepali melodies with Bollywood beats.

Our Current Project: “Unki Kya Baat Karein”

Song Description: “Unki Kya Baat Karein” is a soulful fusion of Hindi and Nepali melodies, featuring Rohan Gurung’s mesmerizing vocals. This bilingual love anthem, produced in collaboration with T-Series, blends traditional instruments and modern Bollywood beats, capturing the essence of a heartfelt, cross-cultural romance.
